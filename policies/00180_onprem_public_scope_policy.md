# On-Prem Public Scope Policy

## Purpose

Define what public `Ops` should include for on-prem users.

## Include

- Kubernetes/Helm generic deployment procedures
- Placeholder-based values and configuration guidance
- Vendor-neutral runbooks for deployment, rollback, and day-2 operations

## Exclude (move to Ops-internal)

- Cloud-vendor specific procedures (GKE/Cloudflare/Artifact Registry)
- Internal environment lanes and promotion paths
- Internal incident records and deployment history
- Internal scripts coupled to company environment matrix
